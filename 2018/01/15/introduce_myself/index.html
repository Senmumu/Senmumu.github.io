Introduce myself

2018-01-15

New Citizen In East of China

When I was a young boy in high school, I image a world under my pen.

When I am an adult struggling in this fascinating world, I image a world with my keyboard.

I was born in a small village in Anhui province of China, and now I am continuing my career in East of China where people call Yangtze Delta, mostly in Shanghai, and at this moment– 01.19.2018, I take place as a data engineer in data department of a middle size company which is focused on spreading infomation in Med and Bio. I deal with half-structured data and text mainly,
and write Python mainly. However, I have some experience in writing D3.js and Vue.js and Boostrap, also Use Golang and Spark usually.

My Study

I have earned a BS degree in Industrial Engineering, and I want to earn a Master degree in applied Math in Tsinghua University, It’s on the top of my list.

I graduated with a middle level GPA, so I shall work hard to get in Tsinghua for a two year master project.

I like reading historical research publications and do some research in History, mainly focus on 1760-1950s Northeast of China, where people called Manchuria.

In engineering field, I am working on Network Science, Graph Theory, Natural Language Processing, and Knowledge Base. and till now I start reading frontier papers and applying them into production.

I also do some reading in Algebraic Geometry which is so brilliant, and am dabbled in Stochastic Process.

My wish list

I’ll meet 24 in lunar new year, and wanna to be a responsible man for job, for family and for my girlfriend.

Now I’m learning Japanese and French, I wish I can speak them freely someday, maybe New Year at next year!
